What is Sahayak?

Sahayak is an intelligent, open-source virtual assistant designed to be your ultimate partner in any digital conversation. Whether you're a sales professional navigating a tough call, a candidate in a crucial job interview, or a manager leading a team meeting, Sahayak provides real-time support directly on your screen. By leveraging advanced AI, it listens to the conversation, identifies who is speaking, and offers timely insights, suggestions, and answers. Sahayak runs securely on your local machine, works with any meeting platform, and ensures you never miss a key detail, empowering you to communicate with greater confidence and clarity.

What Makes Sahayak Different?

Unlike other tools, Sahayak runs locally on your machine, ensuring privacy and performance.

Simple Steps to Get Started

  • 1 Install & Run Follow the simple setup instructions and launch the application in your local environment.
  • 2 Floating Popup Appears A small, non-intrusive popup appears on your screen, ready to assist.
  • 3 Start Recording In your meeting, click 'Record' on the popup to activate the AI assistant.
  • 4 Get Real-time Responses Sahayak starts listening, analyzing the conversation, and provides instant insights directly on your screen.

Core Technology

Built with powerful and modern tools to deliver a seamless AI experience.

AI & ML

Analysis and generation powered by Google's Gemini models.

Speaker Diarization

Using pyannote.audio for accurate, real-time speaker identification.

Speech-to-Text

High-accuracy transcription powered by OpenAI's Whisper.

Backend & Infra

Containerized microservices running on Cloud Run and DigitalOcean.
